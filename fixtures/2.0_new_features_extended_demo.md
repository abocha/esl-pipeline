## 7. ğŸ§  Why This Mission Helps You
You are very logical, but English has two different "logic systems" for these verbs. **System A (Want)** uses "to". **System B (Can)** does not use "to". This homework forces your brain to separate these systems so you don't mix them when speaking to colleagues.
